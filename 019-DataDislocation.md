## Solving Data Dislocation Syndrome

Over time: People have stored data on different devices, operating systems, software platforms,
email hosting systems, contact management systems, business intelligence software and cloud
based social media platforms.
As a result: We are collectively having difficulty accessing and securing our data stored on current
and legacy telephones, laptops, desktops, software, hardware and operating systems.
Industry suffers: Because Software developers struggle with hundreds of disparate data tables and
communication systems developed on poorly elaborated, hard to access, and antiquated software
development platforms that ultimately put users’ information at risk at many levels.
